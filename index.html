ABOUT ME
DOWNLOAD CV

Hi, my name is Liuzixuan (Peter) Lin (Chinese: 林刘子轩, Pronunciation: lin lieu tzi shuan). I'm a CS PhD student at the University of Chicago, and I'm working on the Zero-carbon Cloud project with Prof. Andrew A. Chien as advisor. I'm interested in Data Analytics and Systems.

I got my bachelor in Computer Science from Peking University in 2019, with a double major in Economics.

PROJECTS

Zero-carbon Cloud
The University of Chicago

We are exploring the opportunity of exploiting a growing phenomenon called "stranded power" in power grids to meet the increasing power demands of data centers and reduce their carbon footprint.

2019.10 - CURRENT

Inferring Urban Regions’ Functions Based on Social Media Text Analysis
Peking Univeristy

We extracted information from microblogs (Sina Weibo) with location tags to infer the topics in a region (e.g., food, travel) with NLP methods (e.g. LSTM for sentiment classification). A case study of Beijing was conducted to verify the availability of our method.

Undergraduate Thesis: The implementation of a knowledge graph-based urban geo-semantics mining system (in Chinese, outstanding undergraduate thesis, PKU EECS)
